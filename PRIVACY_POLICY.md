# Privacy Policy — Climate Permit

**Effective date:** May 14, 2026
**App:** Climate Permit (Android)
**Developer:** George Abrahamyants
**Contact:** gugosf@gmail.com

---

## 1. Summary

Climate Permit does not collect, store, transmit, or sell any personal data about you. All quiz answers, archetype results, and compatibility comparisons are processed entirely on your device. Nothing is sent to any server operated by us, because we do not operate any server.

The only data that leaves your device is anonymous, aggregate analytics required by the third-party advertising service that supports this free app (Google AdMob). That collection is governed by Google's policies, not ours, and is described in Section 4.

---

## 2. Information We Collect

**None.** We do not request, collect, or store any of the following:

- Your name, email, phone number, address, or any other identifier
- Your location
- Your contacts, photos, files, or device identifiers
- Your quiz answers, archetype results, or compatibility scores
- Your usage patterns or device characteristics
- Any analytics data of any kind sent to us

The app does not require an account. There is no login. There is no user database.

---

## 3. How Your Quiz Data Is Handled

Quiz answers are stored only in your device's app memory while you are using the app. When you close the app, they are discarded.

If you use the couple compatibility feature, your answers and result are encoded into a shareable link that you choose where to send. We do not see or store this link. The link is processed entirely on the recipient's device when they open it. We do not log any access to these links because we do not operate the link infrastructure.

---

## 4. Third-Party Services

This app uses the following third-party services. Their data collection is governed by their own privacy policies, not ours.

### Google AdMob (advertising)
- Purpose: serve ads that make this free app financially viable
- Data collected by Google: advertising ID, approximate location, IP address, device information, ad interactions
- You can opt out of personalized ads in your device settings:
  - Android: Settings → Google → Ads → "Delete advertising ID" or "Opt out of Ads Personalization"
- Google's privacy policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy
- AdMob's terms: https://support.google.com/admob/answer/6128543

### Google Play Services
- Required by Android to deliver the app
- Google's privacy policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

We do not control, access, or receive any of the data that Google collects through these services.

---

## 5. Permissions

The app requests only the minimum permissions needed:

- **Storage** (Android): required to save your permit card image to your device when you tap "Save" or "Share." We do not read any other files on your device.
- **Internet**: required to display ads via Google AdMob.

The app does NOT request access to: camera, microphone, location, contacts, calendar, SMS, phone, body sensors, or any other sensitive permission.

---

## 6. Children's Privacy

This app is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children under 13. Because the app does not collect any personal information from any user, this restriction is structural — there is nothing to collect.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child is using this app, no action is needed on our end, as no data exists.

---

## 7. Data Retention

We retain no data because we collect no data. There is nothing for you to request access to, correct, export, or delete from us. Your device's app storage can be cleared at any time through Android's Settings → Apps → Climate Permit → Storage → Clear data.

For data collected by Google through AdMob, you must contact Google to exercise any rights. Their tools for doing so are linked above in Section 4.

---

## 8. Your Rights (GDPR / CCPA / Other)

Since we do not collect or process any personal data, GDPR data subject rights and CCPA consumer rights do not apply to us in any operational sense. We have no data to provide, correct, port, or delete.

For data Google collects through AdMob, exercise your rights through Google directly.

---

When that happens, we will update the Effective date at the top of this page and update the app description to reflect material changes. Continued use of the app after such an update constitutes acceptance.
