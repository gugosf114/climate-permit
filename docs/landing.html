§ 1

Two axes decide everything

Classification is not a vibe. Your ten settings resolve to a position on a grid: who the cabin is run for, and how hard it is run. The four quadrants hold the sixteen operators.

§ 3

How the permit is issued

1.A
Operate the controls
Set driver temp, fan, vents, recirc, A/C, mode, rear vents, heated seats, pre-cool — exactly as you would in your own car.

Behavior is scored
Your choices resolve to a quadrant on the self/other × controller/chill grid. No opinions are asked. Your hands already told us.

The permit is issued
You receive a California Operator Permit naming your archetype — share it, or test a partner for cabin compatibility.
